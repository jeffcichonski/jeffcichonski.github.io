Our Story

Christina (The Bride) Andrew (The Groom)
Born September 2, 1988. Born August 9th, 1990.

It all started in the summer of 2006 at Wegmans, where Christina and Andrew both worked. Christina was known as “Boss Lady” and remains ever since. They spent many months as friends until July 5th, 2007 when they started officially dating. Christina left for college shortly after at West Chester University. They spent many months driving back and forth to spend time together. Eventually, Christina transferred to a school closer to home, and rejoined Andrew at Wegmans, working together again.

In 2010, Christina and Andrew got their dog, Bentley, and he has been their little troublemaker ever since. Later that year, they moved into an apartment together in Fort Lauderdale, Fl.

On December 13, 2013, Andrew took Christina to Jimmy's Restaurant and Chocolate Shop for dinner, a place they frequent. He surprised her with a table on the balcony, champagne and chocolate covered strawberries, and asked her to marry him. She of course said yes!

Now, Andrew, Christina and Bentley are living in Northern Fort Lauderdale, planning their wedding. They feel like they are already living the married life, yet can’t wait to make it official on April 18th, 2015! They hope you can be there to celebrate with them!
